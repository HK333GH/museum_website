WELCOME TO THE MUSEUM

I entered the Web3 space in 2021 and quickly became fascinated by NFTs — first drawn to historically significant pieces like CryptoPunks and CurioCards, then discovering the boundless creativity of generative art on Ethereum through platforms like Art Blocks. Later, I found a wealth of innovative works on the Tezos blockchain via fxHash.

This museum is my way of sharing some of my favourite pieces with the public.
